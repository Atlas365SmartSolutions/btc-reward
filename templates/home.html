Simple FAQ

Clear boundaries for a merchant-funded reward product.

Is this guaranteed yield?

No. Rewards are variable, merchant-funded, and depend on merchant rules, BTC price movement, and approval.

Does the customer own the merchant's Bitcoin?

No. The customer receives a reward receipt. The merchant controls its own Bitcoin and chooses whether to approve rewards.

What can rewards be paid as?

The demo shows sats equivalent, but merchants could also use store credit or fiat equivalent.

What is mocked in this POC?

Payments, payout execution, and wallet transfer are mocked. The demo focuses on the reward math, receipt flow, and customer experience.
